6. Google Drive Backup (Optional Feature)

Birthday Reminder offers an optional Google Drive backup feature that allows you to:

6.1 What Data is Backed Up

  • Names and dates of birthday reminders you create
  • This data is stored as a CSV file in your personal Google Drive

6.2 How It Works

  • You must explicitly enable this feature and sign in with your Google account
  • The app uses Google Sign-In to authenticate you
  • Birthday data is stored in your personal Google Drive (not on our servers)
  • Only you have access to this data through your Google account
  • You can disable backup at any time from the app settings
  • You can delete the backup file from your Google Drive at any time

6.3 Third-Party Services
